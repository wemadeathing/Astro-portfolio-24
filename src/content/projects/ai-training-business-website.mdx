## The Challenge

**Market Education**
People need practical AI skills, not just theoretical knowledge about what AI can do.

**Personal Brand**
Establishing credibility in the AI training space while building my own expertise publicly.

**Workshop Booking System**
Built a workshop booking system where users can book and receive a confirmation email.

**Managing Workshop Bookings**
By prompting Claude I am able to manage the Neon database with ease.

## Role & Responsibilities
- Full-stack development and design
- Business strategy and positioning
- Brand design and development
- Email marketing and automation setup

## Key Features

### Workshop Services
Modern responsive design and booking system:

- **User Experience**: Clean, focused interface that prioritizes navigation over flashy features
- **Practical Focus**: Content emphasizes real-world application rather than theoretical AI concepts
- **Modern Architecture**: Astro with React islands for optimal performance and developer experience
- **Database Management**: Neon DB for managing workshops and registrations

## Results & Impact

**Active Business**
Successfully launched with ongoing participant enrollment and positive feedback on practical approach.

**Technical Demonstration**
Platform itself demonstrates the quality possible when using AI tools effectively for development.

**Content Flexibility**
MDX-based content system allows rapid updates and interactive workshop information.

**Professional Growth**
Established credibility in AI education while expanding full-stack development skills.
